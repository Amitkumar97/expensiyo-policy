Frequently Asked Questions

What is Expensiyo?

Expensiyo is a smart cashbook and expense tracker app for managing income and expenses effortlessly. It’s offline-ready, supports multiple languages, dark mode, and secure Google Drive backup.

Which platforms support Expensiyo?

Expensiyo is currently available on Android via Google Play Store.

Is my data safe on Expensiyo?

Yes! Expensiyo respects your privacy. All data stays on your device, with optional secure Google Drive backup. No personal data is collected.

Do I need to create an account to use Expensiyo?

No, Expensiyo works entirely without sign-ups. Your data remains private and local unless you choose to back it up to Google Drive.
